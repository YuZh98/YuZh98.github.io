If you work in Bayesian statistics and use R, one page is worth bookmarking:

> **[CRAN Task View: Bayesian Inference](https://cran.r-project.org/web/views/Bayesian.html)**


A **CRAN Task View** is a curated, expert-maintained guide to the R packages for a particular topic. The **Bayesian Inference** view catalogs 200+ packages for Bayesian computation and organizes them by purpose.

It is maintained by Jong Hee Park, Michela Cameletti, Xun Pang, and Kevin M. Quinn, and is revised regularly (the current version is dated March 16, 2026), so it stays a reliable snapshot of the ecosystem rather than a one-off list.
